**Stay Safe Online**

* Never share your OTP, UPI PIN or passwords with anyone.  
* Don’t click on unknown links or messages.  
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for payments or important logins.  
* Keep your apps and phone updated for better security.  
* Don’t try random payment requests on UPI apps.  
* Use strong passwords and don’t use the same password everywhere.  
* Think twice before posting personal information on social media.

**UPI Safety:**

* You don’t need to enter UPI PIN to receive money.  
* Always check the name before sending money to anyone.  
* If any fraud happens, report immediately.  
* National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in  
* Helpline Number: 1930
