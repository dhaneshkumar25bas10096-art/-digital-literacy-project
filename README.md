Communication in digital spaces has become an important part of student life. Emails,  
discussion forums, messaging apps, and professional platforms are now used daily for  
academic and career-related interactions. Despite learning basic rules of professional

**TASK-5**  
Online fraud is a common cybercrime in India as well as all over the World. where  
attackers or hackers pretend users are sending money through digital payment apps.  
This usually starts when a scammer contacts the victim through phone, SMS, social  
media, pretending to be a bank official, customer support agent, or even a seller. The  
scammer then sends a fake payment request or a malware link and asks the victim to  
check it . If the users touch it then the information and data are transferred to them.  
UPI payment fraud is one of the most common cybercrimes happening in India  
nowadays. Many people don’t even know how easily they can get fooled.  
The process involves confusing the victim. For example, the attacker may claim that  
money is being transferred and the user must enter a PIN to receive it. However,  
entering the UPI PIN actually authorizes a payment instead of receiving money.
